Rapids! Make Memories!

Our goal is to provide you with the best experience possible. We have been providing unforgettable adventures for over 30 years. We are a family owned and operated business that is dedicated to providing you with the safest and most exciting white water rafting trips.

History

Rapids! started floating the river in 1995. What began as a small weekend activity with a single raft has grown into a full–service adventure company. Our experienced guides know every twist and turn of the river and are dedicated to providing safe, exciting, and memorable experiences for every guest.

Today we operate multiple rivers and difficulty levels, from gentle family floats to world–class white water. Generations of guests return year after year to share the river with their families and friends, creating memories that last a lifetime.
